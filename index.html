    Задание по словообразованию: Префиксы, Суффиксы и Корни

    1. Prefixes: A prefix is a group of letters added at the beginning of a word to change its meaning. For example, in the word "unhappiness," "un-" is a prefix. Identify the prefixes in the following words and determine their meanings:

    • Unhappy
    • Disagree
    • Preheat
    • Submarine
    • Misunderstand

    2. Suffixes: A suffix is a group of letters added at the end of a word to change its form or part of speech. For example, in the word "teacher," "-er" is a suffix. Identify the suffixes in the following words and determine their meanings:

    • Careless
    • Hopeful
    • Runner
    • Quickly
    • Happiness

    3. Roots: A root is the main part of a word that carries its core meaning. For example, in the word "happily," "hap-" is a root. Identify the roots in the following words:

    • Understand
    • Unhappiness
    • Rebuild
    • Forgotten
    • Swimming

    Мои ответы:

    1. U, Dis, Pr, Sub, Misu 2. ss, ful, er, ly,ss 3. stand, hap, build, gotten, wimmi
